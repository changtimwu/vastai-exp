## Notes

- One-shot, single seed (42). Not a multi-seed average.
- VRAM was tight: cudaMalloc failed when allocating a 2633 MiB CUDA compute
  buffer on device 1. Allocation eventually succeeded with smaller buffer, but
  this is the danger zone — Q4_K_S or one fewer offloaded layer would buy headroom.
- `llama-cli` ignored `-no-cnv` / `</dev/null` and looped in chat-REPL mode
  reading EOF. The benchmark numbers above came out before the loop ran away.
  Future runs should use `llama-completion` (now the default in `bench.py`).
