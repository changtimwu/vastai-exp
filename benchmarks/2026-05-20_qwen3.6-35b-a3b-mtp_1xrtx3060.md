## Comparison to source (#1)

The source post on the same Q6_K_XL + MTP config + 12 GB RTX 3060 reported
**45.1 – 47.6 tok/s** generation across four workloads. We measured **17.4 tok/s**
— roughly **2.5× slower** than the source.

Likely culprits (we can't isolate without more runs):

- **Host CPU / RAM bandwidth dominate at `-ncmoe 42`**: 42 MoE layers are
  executed on the CPU. The source used a 64 GB DDR5 desktop; this vast.ai
  slice gets 12 vCPU on a Xeon (DDR4) and ~63 GB host RAM. DDR4 vs DDR5
  bandwidth alone is ~1.5–2×, and Xeon DDR4 is on a NUMA-shared host so
  effective bandwidth to one container is lower still.
- **llama.cpp commit**: source built from `b9117-ebe4fca4b`; we built master
  at `b1-871b0b7`. Recent MTP changes may have affected throughput.
- **Draft acceptance**: source reported 0.61 – 0.80 — we didn't capture per-run
  acceptance rate. Worth adding `--verbose` to next run.
